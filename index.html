What is SYNCHRONIZED?

Not a Group.
A Family.

We don’t look for the strongest individuals — we build strong bonds. This isn’t just a group, it’s FAMILY. Respect comes first. Loyalty matters. We grow together, move together, and win together.

No ego. No drama. Just people who value teamwork, trust, and genuine connection. We believe strength isn’t measured by skill alone, but by how well we support each other on and off the game floor.

We require 21 years old and above only — because maturity, accountability, and respect matter to us.

If you’re looking for a place where you belong, where synchronization is more than a name — this might be your home.
